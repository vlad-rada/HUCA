About

HACU, the Human Adoption Centre of the Universe, is an organization dedicated to the preservation and adoption of humans, universewide. Humans are one of the most interesting pets that an individual can keep. Be warned, however, that maintaining humans does come with dangers, and should be researched thoroughly before attempting to adopt. This is expanded upon on other pages of this website.


History

The history behind this organization originates one fateful day when our head researchers, Gjzir Kmrissi and Jeisk Hriksk, were exploring the depths of the universe in their spaceship equipped with never before seen technology. They came upon a small and dying planet, brought to ruin by the inhabitants' own carelessness. They found it adorable and took plenty home to show their team. Soon enough, HACU was created in order to assist with the proper care and distribution of this species, as well as to keep the species from harming itself further.
